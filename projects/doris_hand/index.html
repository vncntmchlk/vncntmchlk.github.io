Doris' Hand

Live-electronics performance in collaboration with dancer/violinist iSaAc EH (2018-)

Since 2018 iSaAc EH and i have been working with diy motion sensors to create a performance piece, where the performer is controlling the sound with their motion. It has been an ongoing collaboration. A first version premiered at the NOW! Festival in Essen (2018), with later performances at the HoerenSehen Festival in Bremen and at the KunstKulturKirche in Frankfurt (both 2019).
